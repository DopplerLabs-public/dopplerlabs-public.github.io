Control your Here Buds™ with the Here One app. Stream music, control Real-World Volume, and use Smart Noise Filters to reduce unwanted noise or amplify speech. The Here One app is the essential companion for your Here Buds.

Here One is designed for iOS & Android.

Here One is optimized for iPhone 5 and above running iOS 9.0 and above (iOS 10.0 and above recommended).

The Android app is optimized for phones running Android 7.0 and above.
